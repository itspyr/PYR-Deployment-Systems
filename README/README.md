# PYR | Prioritze Your Risk

PYR is a lightweight front end MVP focused on building trading discipline and decision quality, not signals.

The product emphasizes:
- structured thinking
- risk awareness
- behavioral consistency
- reducing impulsive and emotional trading

This MVP is intentionally simple and runs entirely on static files with local persistence.

---

## Core Features

### App (Plan Builder)
- Structured trade plan inputs
- Behavioral warnings
- Simple risk framing
- Designed to slow users down before execution

---

### Challenge My Trade (Coach Engine)
An open ended trade review tool that:
- Analyzes written trade plans
- Detects behavioral language such as FOMO, revenge, hope, and overconfidence
- Scores the plan across multiple dimensions
- Surfaces risks, missing pieces, and do nothing warnings
- Flashes when meaningful coach feedback changes

The coach output is deterministic and rules based. No predictions and no signals.

---

### Daily Training
- 5 questions per day
- 1 attempt per day
- Same question set all day (date seeded)
- Points and streaks
- Designed to reinforce reasoning and discipline, not memorization

---

### Account
- Session style account view
- Saved settings
- Points and streak tracking
- Stored locally (MVP phase)
